KNOW ABOUT OUR LABORTORIES

Welcome to our vibrant laboratory facilities, the beating heart of scientific exploration at Purna Vidya Bhavan. Here, students dive into hands-on learning experiences that bring science to life. Our well-equipped labs provide the perfect setting for students to conduct experiments, analyze data, and unleash their creativity.

Individualized Guidance

In our labs, every student receives individualized guidance from our experienced faculty members. Whether it's conducting experiments, analyzing data, or troubleshooting, our faculty is dedicated to supporting students at every step, fostering a sense of confidence and independence.

Inspiring the Next Generation of Scientists

Purna Vidya Bhavan's laboratory faculty is dedicated to inspiring the next generation of scientists, researchers, and innovators. Through their guidance and mentorship, students are empowered to pursue careers in science and make meaningful contributions to the world.

Biology Lab

Discover the Wonders of Life

Welcome to our Biology Lab, where the mysteries of life unfold before your eyes. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and an array of specimens, students embark on a journey of exploration and discovery. From dissecting frogs to observing microscopic organisms, every experiment is a hands-on experience that brings textbooks to life. Our dedicated faculty guide students through practical exercises, fostering a deep understanding of biological concepts. Step into our lab and immerse yourself in the captivating world of biology.

Chemistry Lab

World of Chemical Reactions

Step into our Chemistry Lab, where reactions come to life and discoveries abound. With state-of-the-art equipment and expert guidance, students delve into the fascinating realm of atoms and molecules. From mixing chemicals to observing colorful reactions, every experiment is an opportunity to understand the fundamental principles of chemistry. Our lab sessions are designed to be interactive and engaging, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios. Join us in the Chemistry Lab and unlock the secrets of the molecular world.

COMPUTER LAB

Step into the future of technology in our Computer Lab

where students harness the power of digital tools and programming languages. With access to high-speed computers, software suites, and coding resources, students dive into the realms of programming, web development, and digital design. From writing algorithms to creating multimedia presentations, the Computer Lab is a space for innovation, creativity, and problem-solving.
